French Toast

Dessert or Breakfast? Does it really matter? Is it even French?

Rich, sweet and a fan favorite. French Toast is as simple to make as it is to eat.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2/3 Cup of milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg
  • Vanilla Extract

*When choosing bread type go with something that's thick and soaks up liquid easily

Instructions

  1. Beat together egg, milk, salt, desired spices and vanilla.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides. Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden. Serve hot.
